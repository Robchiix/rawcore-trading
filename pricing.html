PRICING FAQ
Can I really cancel anytime?
Yes. No questions, no retention offers, no dark patterns. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period, then it stops. Simple.
Is the weekly plan the same as monthly?
Almost. You get all the same bots, lessons, signals and community access. The only things excluded from weekly are the Core Pot entry and live build sessions — those are monthly-only perks.
What payment methods do you accept?
We're setting up Stripe — card payments, Apple Pay, Google Pay. More options coming. If you want to join before payments are live, reach out on the contact page.
Is there a free trial for Core?
The Observer tier is essentially a permanent free trial — you get a real taste of the content and community before spending anything. The weekly plan is also a low-risk way to try everything.
Do prices include tax?
Prices shown are exclusive of VAT where applicable. Your checkout will show the final amount before you confirm anything.
Will prices go up?
Possibly as the platform grows — but existing members always keep their current rate. We'd rather reward loyalty than punish it.
