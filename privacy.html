// 01
OVERVIEW

RawCore Trading ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

We are data controllers under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our platform is operated from Ireland and data is stored within the European Union.

// 02
WHAT WE COLLECT
DATA TYPE WHAT WHY
Account data Username, email address To create and manage your account
Authentication Google/Telegram OAuth tokens To verify your identity securely
Billing data Payment method (handled by Stripe — we never see full card details) To process subscription payments
Usage data Pages visited, lessons completed, credits earned To provide platform features and track progress
Community content Messages, bot submissions, trade ideas you post To operate the community features
Technical data IP address, browser type, device type For security and platform improvement

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the platform. We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial account details, government IDs, or health information.

// 03
HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the RawCore Trading platform
  • Managing your account and membership
  • Processing payments and managing subscriptions
  • Sending service-related communications (account updates, billing notices)
  • Operating the community and credits system
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing abuse

We do not: sell your data, use it for advertising, share it with third parties for their marketing, or use it to make automated decisions that significantly affect you.

// 04
WHO WE SHARE DATA WITH

We share data only with trusted service providers necessary to operate the platform:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting. Data stored in EU region.
  • Vercel — website hosting. Standard web server logs only.
  • Google/Telegram — if you use social login, their OAuth policies apply.

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties. We will disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

// 05
DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Your data is stored on secure servers within the European Union. We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong password and notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorised access to your account.

// 06
COOKIES

We use only essential cookies necessary to operate the platform — session cookies to keep you logged in and security tokens. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings but this may affect platform functionality, particularly login features.

// 07
YOUR RIGHTS

Under GDPR you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain uses of your data
  • Restriction — request we restrict processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@rawcoretrading.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission at dataprotection.ie.

// 08
CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

RawCore Trading is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has registered, please contact us and we will delete their data immediately.

// 09
CONTACT US

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: hello@rawcoretrading.com
  • Telegram: t.me/rawcoretrading

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify active members of significant changes via email. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
