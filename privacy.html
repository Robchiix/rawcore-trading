WHO WE ARE

RawCore Trading operates at rawcoretrading.com. For privacy matters, contact us at hello@rawcoretrading.com.

DATA WE COLLECT

Account data: Email address, name (if provided via Google OAuth), and profile information when you register.

Usage data: Lessons completed, tasks answered, credits earned, and platform activity — used to power your progress tracking and leaderboard.

Waitlist data: Email address if you join our waitlist, used only to notify you of launch updates.

Payment data: Processed securely via Stripe. We do not store card details on our servers.

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

To provide and improve platform services. To send transactional emails (account, billing, launch notifications). To display your progress and leaderboard position. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely via Supabase (EU region). Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth with industry-standard encryption. Payment processing is handled by Stripe.

YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

You have the right to: access your personal data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion of your data, export your data, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, email hello@rawcoretrading.com.

COOKIES

We use essential cookies only — for authentication sessions and platform functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. No cookie consent banner needed.

CONTACT

Privacy concerns: hello@rawcoretrading.com
